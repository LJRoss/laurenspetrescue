All About Oreo!

Age & Breed

Oreo is a 3 year old Australian Shepherd mix.

Personality

Oreo is a rambunctious pup! He is very high energy and playful. he gets along well wit other dogs and makes fast friends with our volunteers.
His favorite things to do are go on walks, play with his Kong toy, and swim!

Additional Info

He is house trained and knows the basic commands sit, stay, and lay down.
He is slightly afraid of cats but get along well dogs of all ages and size.
